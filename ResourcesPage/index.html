Here are some of my favorite or most recommended resources!

For building a visual brand

  • Coolors- Generates color palettes.

  • Huemint - Generates color palettes for web design specifically

  • Canva - Make logos, banners, posters, and more.

General sites to host your work

  • Carrd - link your info and other social media using this microsite builder.

  • Wix - Generates color palettes for web design specifically

For inspiration and references

  • Dribbble - a publishing site that displays others portfolio work. Specifically made for UI/UX designers and researchers, but makes for a good reference in general.

  • Medium - hosts articles from already established and experienced people that do their own guides.
