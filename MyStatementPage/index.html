My Statement

This site was made thanks to the help of online guides and done using primarily HTML and CSS.

I'm no stranger to the internet, like most people my age, but a lot of people have trouble putting themselves out there, and I totally understand. It's a part of you, and sometimes a very vulnerable part as well. I have nothing but respect for people who create for themselves.

I've always had trouble trying to establish myself, so I made this guide to help others. The internet is vast, and not everyone knows where to start, so I would just like to lend a simple hand as well as dish out my own personal expeirences.
